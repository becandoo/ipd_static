Product Description

 

Potential Product Fitment Issues

Direct fit over 2004-2007 S60R & V70R models with big brakes - no spacers required.

Volvo P1 chassis S40, V50 C70 & C30 models require a lug nut update when running our Eros wheels. See recommomended products tab for compatible Gorilla brand lug nuts.

The Volvo OEM style lug nuts used with Volvo OEM alloy wheels on these models (pictured at right) are NOT recommended for use with IPD wheels.
