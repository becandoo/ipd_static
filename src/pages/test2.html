    Product Description

    Volvo has certainly become famous (or infamous) for the refined and civil presentation of their vehicles on the road. No one has ever accused Volvo of being overly brash in traffic. This is all well and good for the family soccer team or grocery hauler but some drivers want to put a little more bark in their bite. A little growl in your exhaust to replace the familiar Volvo “whisper whoosh”, or maybe just a little “road music” for that inner gear-head.

    Our T5 exhaust kit is beautiful and functional. This 304 stainless system is mandrel bent and hand welded for an impeccable bolt-in fit. The system mounts in the stock locations and will cause no errors or malfunctions to seamlessly preserve your civil drive. The note is pleasant on the street and audible from inside the car. You certainly won’t offend the neighbors with this system, but you may just smile a bit more on that morning commute.

    Our catback performance exhaust kit is a direct bolt-on to the stock downpipe. The stock exhaust will require cutting for removal but the catback is a simple 45 minute install with hand tools.

    This exhaust kit is supplied with round slash cut tips which directly match models through 2013. These tips do fit later models but may not completely fill the valance openings. Alternative valances with different openings are available from Volvo for most models if desired.

    2017-09-04 - "RWP" from WATERBURY CT

    First off, I have to say that I've done a few exhaust change outs in my lifetime ('88 Chevy Cavalier, '97 GMC Sonoma, '94 Ford Explorer, '86 Ford Mustang, '00 Ford Explorer) and this change out was the easiest to do. Fitment was perfect for every piece of pipe supplied, and being able to adjust the angle of the tailpipe was very nice to get the look that I was going for. Tonal sound coming from the exhaust is very nice and is not overpowering. But when you do get into it, you definitely will notice the sound. Get some good 3/8" stainless steel bolts, washers(4), lock washers(2) and nuts for the install. None were supplied with the kit. Other than that snafu, I am really pleased with this product.
