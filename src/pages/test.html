Product Description

Volvo OEM brake pads.


Potential Product Fitment Issues

140, 164, 1800 and 240/260 owners will need to check which brand rear calipers your car is equipped with. These pads only work with ATE branded calipers.

There is no way to know for sure what calipers are on your car without visual inspection. It is very common on these older cars for the original calipers to have been replaced by a different brand. We've even seen cars with mismatched left and right branded calipers (which take different pads.)

Fits all 740/760/780 models that do not have Independent Rear Suspension

Fits all 940/960 models that do not have Independent Rear Suspension

Fits FWD (Front Wheel Drive) S70 & V70 models 1998-2000 (does NOT fit AWD models)

Fits all 1998-2004 C70 models.
