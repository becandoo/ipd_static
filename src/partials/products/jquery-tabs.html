Product Description

Over a year in development and testing went into our revolutionary stainless steel oval tube sport exhuast system. It's the first of its kind to address concerns with other available sport systems. 

The oval tubing concept comes straight out of NASCAR, where itâ€™s used to provide maximum ground clearance and reduced wind drag. We went to great lengths to develop a system featuring telescoping slip joints in key areas to allow fitment on both the S60R and the V70R. This helped reduce cost as well as making resale of the systems in the future much more attractive.

The oval tubing used in our system has an equivalent cross section of 3-1/8" diameter round tubing that offers excellent flow characteristics and the compact stainless rear muffler uses long life rock wool for an aggressive tone. This exhaust carries a tuned sound letting you know the exhaust has been modified. The sound is aggressive under load and heavy throttle, but not obnoxious when cruising. Expect performance gains between 14-16hp.

Features:

  • Equivalent ground clearance compared to stock system
  • Oval Tubing construction provides flow equivalent to 3 1/8" round tubing.
  • Stainless Steel Construction
  • High Volume Low Restriction Rock Wool Muffler
  • Telescoping design allows for precise tailpipe fitment for S60R and V70R
  • Includes required install hardware -- clamps + downpipe-to-catback gasket + bolts/nuts/washers

Note: Exhaust is sold as a complete unit.  Individual components are not sold separately.
